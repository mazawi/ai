Teaching & supervision

Learning grounded in theory and applied to real problems

My teaching philosophy is based on connecting conceptual understanding with practical application. Students should not simply learn techniques; they should understand why a method works, when it should be used and how it can be evaluated critically.

I have taught across computer engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence and related computing areas in Oman, Jordan and Iraq. My academic work also includes supervision and external examination of PhD theses.
