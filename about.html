Academic profile

A career connecting research and institutional development

Dr Mohammad Al-Azawi is an Associate Professor of Artificial Intelligence with more than 24 years of higher education experience. He has served academic institutions in Oman, Jordan and Iraq and has held senior academic roles including Deputy Dean, Assistant Dean, Acting Dean and Head of Department.

His research interests include computational intelligence, computer vision, machine learning, medical image analysis and data science. His work is particularly associated with human-inspired machine vision, computational saliency and symmetry-based brain abnormality identification.

His administrative experience spans academic affairs, research and innovation, quality assurance, accreditation, strategic planning, curriculum development and the design of institutional policies and manuals.

Teaching philosophy

My educational philosophy centres on the holistic integration of research, teaching and community engagement. I believe that research should generate knowledge, teaching should translate knowledge into applied competence, and external engagement should use that knowledge to create meaningful value for society and industry.
